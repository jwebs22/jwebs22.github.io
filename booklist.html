The Theater Audition Book
Amazon Link

Author: Gerald Lee Ratliff.

Has an index that seperates out monologues by gender, type and time period. Didn't have a ton for college auditions, but some usable stuff in there. Might serve you well beyond college.
Before I GOT IN! The Young Teen's College Audition Guide For Acting And Musical Theatre
Amazon Link

Author: Mary Anna Dennard.

Ideally you read this when your kid is a Freshman in HS. But it's still full of good info if you are coming in late to the game.
"In this prequel to her popular I GOT IN! series, Dennard offers guidance for families of high school freshmen and sophomores on how to prepare early for the highly competitive college audition process. Included is a college audition timeline and how to research college programs. Also included is a list of summer performing arts programs and colleges."
I GOT IN! The Ultimate College Audition Guide For Acting And Musical Theater
Amazon Link

SAuthor: Mary Anna Dennard.

"In this UPDATED 2021 EDITION, nationally recognized college audition coach, Mary Anna Dennard, shares her expertise on how to prepare for the highly competitive college audition process. Included are: Tips on the newly important prescreens, exclusive audition tips from college auditors, list of colleges with performing arts degrees, guidance on monologues, songs, wardrobe, head shot and resume, how to schedule college auditions."
COLLEGE AUDITION WORKBOOK: A step-by-step guide through the college audition process!

Authors: CAP College Audition Project
